About Us

GreenByte is a website dedicated to bridging the gap between innovation and sustainability, with a special focus on IT students. Our mission is simple yet powerful: to raise awareness about how individuals in the tech industry can unintentionally contribute to environmental issues and more importantly, how they can become part of the solution.
